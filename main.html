Harry Potter and the Philosopher's Stone

Harry Potter and the Philosopher’s Stone (2001) takes audiences on a magical adventure as an 11-year-old boy, Harry Potter, discovers he is no ordinary child but a wizard with a destiny. Upon receiving an invitation to study at the prestigious Hogwarts School of Witchcraft and Wizardry, Harry steps into a world of spells, mythical creatures, and hidden secrets. Alongside his newfound friends, Ron Weasley and Hermione Granger, he unravels the mystery of the Sorcerer’s Stone, a legendary artifact that grants immortality. As he navigates his first year at Hogwarts, Harry learns about his parents' past, his connection to the dark wizard Lord Voldemort, and the true meaning of bravery and friendship. Directed by Chris Columbus and based on J.K. Rowling’s beloved novel, the film captures the enchantment and wonder of the wizarding world, setting the stage for an iconic cinematic journey.
